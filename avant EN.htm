What fed Tiemko
Compositions on piano, vibraphone, drums, announcing Tiemko were recorded on revox, also cassette and were elaborated in a vast duplex that had served as a studio for Sonia Delaunay, Éric's grandmother, in Paris 7ème, rue Saint-Simon, by Toussaint and Delaunay – who met in 1976 to form the group Phénix (Jean-Jacques was a bass player back then). They spent whole afternoons devoted to listening ia King Crimson, Genesis, Zappa, Gentle Giant, Art Zoyd, Deep Purple, Queen or Shostakovich, Bartok, Dutilleux, Messiaen, Stravinsky... Chauvidan played in the groups Les Faucons Blancs (image below),
Desdemona, Doctor Mad, had a first studio experience with singer Bevinda – who introduced him to literary and theater man †Gilles Canuto, with whom a great complicity was established as regards literature, sport, theater or staging, raving about on impossible texts, writing improbable songs – then Marie Grenon (see Marie Faye), whom he accompanied in concert with Jean-François "Framboise" Bouvier. The keyboardist composer and arranger François Bréant stired Rémy's curiosity for the universes of Zappa, Varese, Charles Yves, Messiaen... In parallel to Tiemko, Rémy took lessons in a school of jazz while working on soundtracks, for cinema, documentaries or animation...
