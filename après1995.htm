Each one of the three leaders would compose a non harmonized melodic line and submit it to the other two, summing up to nine pieces. A tenth, which would have been the synthesis (according to the principle of the "exquisite corpse") was the only one left uncomposed. This successful project still remains today an appreciated item in the world of prog rock. Recordings in Brest, mixing in Metz, production in Paris and three different musical worlds confer to this album, a unique variety and richness.
1996 DeBoCo – Éric Delaunay's last achievements
Tiemko did not rule out reforming, but a nasty illness claimed Eric's life in July 1996 and prevented it for good. This left DeBoCo unfinished, Rémy completed the existing pieces as a producer and musician. In order to gain flexibility, also because Tiemko's disbanding, leaders would be on charge rather than bands : Eric Delaunay of course, Gilles Coppin (Halloween keyboardist), and Jean-Pascal Boffo – Thierry Payssan having finally renounced.
1998 remix, posthumous publishing
R. Chauvidan et JJ. Toussaint remixed Espace Fini in 1998, each one of them comosing from a theme by Eric Delaunay they'd played à la Pinte but had not planned to record (Eric didn't like it !), finally Marie Grenon sung on a recording along with baryton Benoît Méjean, soprano Bévinda, drummer François Verly. En 2003 improvisation excerpts recorded in a rehearsal studio at rue Amelot back in 1994, along with a piece by JJ. Toussaint composed in 1996, were published on CD "Ça Tourne...", it has a 1990 live video by Alain Tyr, that seems to have remained the only one of that kind – several other VHS vids would be considered garbage when Delaunay's flat was emptied after he'd passed away – and an animation by Jean-Michel Brière on the piece named Clone.
2006 Travelling
Toussaint's solo project Travelling has rare moments that'd remind of Tiemko. It has "Volutes", a piece by Rémy who presented him to Pip Pyle after rue St Maur, studio "La Station" en 1997 – rehearsals for Travelling with this musically and humanly most appreciable drummer, one month before studio recording ! Great player François Verly became the one on drums. Du fait que les compositions de Travelling ne sont plus contraintes par un nombre fixe de musiciens (d'autant moins qu'aucun concert, plus simple à organiser pour un trio, n'est envisagé), elles sont beaucoup plus fournies en instrumentation et le champ d'exploration sonore s'en trouve élargi.
2012 Droit de Rêve
Musicalement, cet album qui met en musique la "poésie sonore" de Jean-Pierre Bobillot qui en est l'auteur des textes, ne rappelle pas particulièrement Tiemko mais une partie des paroles de "Célimène" rappelle l'univers de la chanson de Clone, "Rock'n'Roll Alice", qui joue avec le même type de rimes.
