RÉMY CHAUVIDAN 1966
Back in 1982, one year before his baccalaureate A7 (Plastic Arts) at the Pablo Picasso high school and several concerts with Desdemona, a hard rock group, Rémy released his first musical creations and went on stage as an actor during the reading of a Grimm tale by the Choucas band association.
In 1984 Rémy played in concert with the rock group Les Faucons Blancs (until 1985) which recorded at the Bénichou studio with, among others, Marie Faye (Prix du public du Chorus des Hauts de Scène, for whom Rémy will become a guitarist since 1992; she participated in the Toulouse Musical Comedy), Jean-Yves D'Angelo, Jean-Jacques Kerléo and Bruno Fatta, and Bevinda for whom he composed, and whose group auditioned, played in concert, performed (until 1987) , has one foot in the variety studio, enjoys beautiful encounters with Nadjat, Pierre Saya, Natalie Kotka
In 1995 Rémy is on the synth and the microphone for the choirs, in the group Doctor Mad (rock), for concerts and appearance on MTV (CafConc’) with Jean-Jacques Kerléo, Christophe Crouset and Laurent Hipper. The same year and until 1997 he was guitarist for Didier Thomanne at auditions and concerts, at the Flèche d'or and other places of musical jousting
From 1996 to 1998 he was an animator and musician at the Audiscène association, participated in the organization of a monthly open stage in Levallois and three stages at the Fête de la Musique.
From 1993 to 2003 Rémy composed music for films, reports and short films in his home studio (see in particular the Canal+ soundtrack edition, the film C'est le pays joyeux des enfants heureux (1998).
From 1997 to 2003 he was a sound engineer in a studio, at the Station association, where Pip Pyle and Philippe Otema, were among the players. Requiem and post-Scriptum, by Tiemko, and Ça Tourne, were recorded there with François Verly, Bévinda, Marie Faye & Jean-Jacques Toussaint.
We owe him an important photographic treasure since his baccalaureate – became a gallery pro photographer for three years. From 1999 to 2020 he was responsible for the copy shop Copy Shop (Paris 8). A sea lover, he owns a boat, the Valparaiso; he's also fond of hiking (a hiking dream came true in Nepal !), otherwise he's a skier, reader, art lover and passionate rider!
